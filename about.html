About Opésh Overseas

~From India to the World

Our Story

Founded with the vision of bridging India’s ancient wellness heritage with modern scientific precision, Opésh Overseas is a premium export venture specializing in Ayurvedic dietary supplements and phytochemical formulations. Our name blends the heritage of our founder's lineage with a global outlook, symbolizing trust, purity, and purpose.

With a commitment to authenticity, Opésh Overseas was born not just as a business, but as a movement to globalize Ayurveda-backed wellness. We carefully select plant-based actives, avoid harmful excipients, and focus on evidence-backed outcomes.

Our Mission

To deliver trusted, clinically oriented plant-based healthcare solutions from India to the world—contributing to a future where natural science and modern research work hand-in-hand.

Our Values

At Opésh Overseas, we value:

  • Integrity – Transparent sourcing, honest communication, ethical practices.
  • Innovation – Formulations based on synergy algorithms and phytochemistry.
  • Quality – Adherence to GMP, ISO, and global compliance standards.
  • Sustainability – Eco-friendly packaging and responsible sourcing.

Leadership

Pavitra Sharma, Founder of Opésh Overseas, leads the organization with a modern entrepreneurial mindset rooted in cultural values. Inspired by a legacy of wellness and trade, his vision is to make high-quality Indian wellness products globally trusted and accessible.

Why Choose Us?

Our operations are designed to meet international demands, offering:

  • Clinically validated formulations
  • FDA-compliant manufacturing partners
  • Custom relabeling for international private labeling
  • Efficient logistics & transparent documentation
