Our Story

Opésh Overseas was born from a deep belief that nature holds the key to preventive healthcare. Our journey began in India with a vision to make Ayurvedic wisdom globally accessible through modern, science-backed formulations. From humble beginnings, we are now building a globally recognized brand committed to ethical, plant-based wellness solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver phytochemistry-based, excipient-free dietary supplements that are safe, effective, and compliant with international health standards. We aim to bridge ancient wellness with modern science, ensuring that every product we export contributes to a healthier world.

Our Values

We are driven by a commitment to quality, transparency, and sustainability. Our values include customer trust, ethical governance, scientific validation, and environmental responsibility. Every decision at Opésh Overseas reflects our unwavering dedication to these principles.

Leadership

Led by Pavitra Sharma, a dynamic and purpose-driven entrepreneur, and legally governed under a woman-led sole proprietorship, Opésh Overseas represents a unique balance of youthful vision and solid ethical foundation. Our leadership brings together business acumen, global insight, and compassion to build a brand that lasts.

Why Choose Us

At Opésh Overseas, we don’t just export products—we deliver trust, backed by science and shaped by tradition. Our team ensures that every shipment reflects clinical safety, global certifications, and phytochemical precision. We support private labeling, FDA-compliant partnerships, and fully documented traceability. This positions us as a reliable long-term partner for global buyers in the wellness industry.
