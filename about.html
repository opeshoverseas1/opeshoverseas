About Us

Opésh Overseas is an elite export house born in India with a vision to deliver pure, phytochemistry-based wellness solutions globally. Built on scientific integrity, modern branding, and ancient Ayurvedic wisdom, we aim to redefine what global nutraceutical standards look like.

Founded by Pavitra Sharma, and operating under the umbrella of Sharma and Sons Ventures Co., Opésh Overseas represents a blend of personal heritage, ethical healthcare, and global entrepreneurship.

We focus on exporting dietary supplements that are clinically researched, excipient-free, and rooted in traditional Indian science. Our team is committed to uncompromising quality, transparent practices, and trusted international compliance.

We are here to offer more than products — we offer a promise: purity, performance, and purpose.
