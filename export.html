Export Strategy & Global Vision

At Opésh Overseas, we specialize in the export of scientifically developed, phytochemistry-based dietary supplements rooted in ancient Ayurvedic wisdom. Our aim is to expand India's wellness heritage across global markets by leveraging clinically backed formulations and robust supply chain strategies.

Our Export Process

Our exports are carried out in full compliance with international regulations including FDA, FSSAI, and customs clearance documentation. We ensure all certifications, CoAs, packing labels, and product information are accurate, standardized, and aligned with the importing country’s laws.

Key Markets

Our current focus markets include the United States, Canada, Germany, UAE, and Australia. We have initiated dialogue with importers and distributors in these regions, and are actively onboarding FDA-registered partners capable of relabeling and fulfilling white-label contracts.

Why Work With Us?

  • Fully customizable packaging and private labelling services
  • Ayurvedic and phytochemical synergy-based formulations
  • Clinical outcomes and COAs available upon request
  • FDA-compliant partners with drop-shipping capabilities
  • Responsive CRM and export documentation team

Logistics & Documentation

Our logistics network includes tie-ups with global freight forwarders and clearing agents. We handle all necessary shipping documents including Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Origin, and any phytosanitary or GMP certificates required by the destination country.

Future Outlook

We aim to build a strong base of recurring B2B partnerships while exploring D2C global export opportunities. As the nutraceutical and herbal supplement space grows exponentially, Opésh Overseas positions itself at the intersection of tradition, science, and trust.
