Global Certifications & Compliance Capabilities

Opésh Overseas operates with a robust compliance framework. Through our own registrations and our network of accredited manufacturing partners, we ensure that every shipment meets the most rigorous global standards.

MSME (Udyam) Registration
Government of India registered for credibility and business facilitation.
Import Export Code (IEC)
Authorized for global trade, enabling smooth customs clearance.
FSSAI Compliance
Products sourced from FSSAI-licensed and audited manufacturing facilities.
Sanstha Adhar No. (SAN)
Recognised and granted SAN by government
ISO 22000 & GMP
Manufacturing partners operate in ISO 22000-certified and GMP-compliant units.
HACCP
Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points framework applied to ensure product safety.

*Certain certifications are maintained by our approved manufacturing partners and apply to all products sourced through them. Documentation available upon request to verified importers.*
